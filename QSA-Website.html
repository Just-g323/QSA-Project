Who We Are

A Decade of Aviation Excellence

Founded in 2013, QSA International has grown into a multi-continental organisation operating across the UAE, Morocco, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.

Our foundation is built on a safety-first culture, technical precision, and regulatory excellence. Holding EASA Part-145 and Part-147 approvals, we maintain and train to the highest international standards.

What We Do

Comprehensive Aviation Services

From line maintenance to technical training and consumable supply, QSA delivers end-to-end engineering solutions for airlines and operators worldwide.

Line Maintenance Services

EASA Part-145 approved maintenance covering transit, turnaround, and overnight checks at airports across three continents.

  • Transit & turnaround checks
  • A-check line maintenance
  • Aircraft defect rectification
  • AOG support and recovery
  • Aircraft on ground assistance
  • Pre-delivery inspections

Technical Training

EASA Part-147 approved training programmes for aviation professionals, from initial type ratings to continuation training.

  • Category A & B1/B2 basic training
  • Aircraft type rating courses
  • Continuation training programmes
  • Human factors training
  • Safety management training
  • Customised operator programmes

Consumables & Engineering

Supply of aviation-grade consumables, spare parts, and ground support equipment, with full engineering support services.

  • Aviation consumables supply
  • Rotable spare parts management
  • Ground support equipment
  • Engineering consultancy
  • Technical documentation
  • Logistics & procurement support
